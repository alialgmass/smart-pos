POS SaaS System — Project Plan & Task Breakdown
Stack: Laravel 11 · Vue 3 · Inertia.js · Spatie · Multi-Tenant (Single DB)
Scope: Egyptian Market — Phase 1 | 40 User Stories · 7 Sprints · 14 Weeks · 96 Tasks

### 1. Project Overview
A multi-tenant POS SaaS tailored for the Egyptian market, featuring inventory management, restaurant-specific workflows, customer debt tracking, and offline capabilities.

### 2. Core Modules
- **Foundation & Auth**: Multi-tenant setup, Spatie RBAC.
- **Products & Inventory**: Excel import, low-stock alerts, variants.
- **POS Core**: Cashier interface, payment modal, thermal printing, WhatsApp receipts.
- **Customers & Debts**: Credit sales tracking, loyalty points.
- **Restaurant Orders**: Table map, KDS/Kitchen printing.
- **Reports & Analytics**: Sales dashboard, profit reports, cashier performance.
- **SaaS & Offline**: PWA, local SQLite sync, subscription billing (PayMob integration).

### 3. Key Screens to Build
1. **Public Registration/Landing**: Tenant sign-up and plan selection.
2. **Admin Dashboard**: Analytics, low-stock warnings, and tenant overview.
3. **POS Cashier Interface**: Product grid, cart, and payment modal.
4. **Restaurant Table Map**: Floor plan for order management.
5. **Customer Debt Management**: Tracking outstanding balances and recording payments.
